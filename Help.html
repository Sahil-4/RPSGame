Rock Paper Scissor

How To Play

Tap on Start and Select which type game you want to play
1. Single Player(With Computer) or
2. Two Player(With Your Friend)
After Selecting The Game Mode:
Choose from the given options:
Rock, Paper or Scissor
to choose the option simply tap on that picture.
Rules:
1. Rock can beat Scissor & can be beaten by Paper 2. Scissor can beat Paper & can be beaten by Rock 3. Paper can beat Rock & can be beaten by Scissor

To play with Your Friend:
You need to Fill The Given Fields:
1. Your Name
2. Your Friend Name(Fill Exact Name that your Friend has Entered)
3. Finally input a 3 Digit Number(You and Your Friend both have to input same Number)
Now Enjoy Your Game.
